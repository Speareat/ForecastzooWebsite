What is a prediction ?
For this prediction contest, every participant gives how he thinks the hospital admissions will evolve within 30 days. That is a prediction is just a sequence of numbers, one for each day. You can enter them manually, in a .csv file, or by drawing a line on the canvas. The number entered correspond to the number of new patients coming to hospital due to Covid-19 in Belgium.
If you choose to submit a .csv file, please put one number per line, nothing else.
